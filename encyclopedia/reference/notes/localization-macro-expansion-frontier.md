   `tools/build_localization_macro_expansion_model.py` and checked by
   `tools/validate_localization_macro_expansion_model.py`.

   source macro shapes, `23` display/inventory commands as direct VM

   macros.

   aliases, especially `@DSP_GOODS`, `@DSP_ITEM`, `@DSP_NAME`,
   `@Q_MONEY`, `@GOODSIN_PLAYER`, and `@Q_GOODSFULL`.

   rather than treating them as ordinary text VM commands.
