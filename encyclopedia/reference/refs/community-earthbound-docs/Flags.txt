The following was converted to CCScript by Catador on the PK Hack discord server. They are the flag names found in Mr. Lindblom's floppy disk, as reported here: https://gamehistory.org/earthbound-script-files

"I still don't think these flag names are exactly great but it's what they used for development so better than nothing."
