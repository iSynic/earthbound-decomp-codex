Effects

Effect arguments for control codes like [1F 1E XX XX YY]

    00 - Not entirely sure
    01 - Instant appearance
    02 - Slowly blinks into existence
    03 - Fades in, horizontal lines
    04 - Fades in, vertical lines
    05 - Sprays in to view

    06 - Instant disappearance
    07 - Slowly blinks out of existence
    08 - Fades out, horizontal lines
    09 - Fades out, vertical lines
    0A - Sprays out of view
    0B - This and anything after it seem to break the ROM; input locks up

Generated Sprites

Generated sprites are made by the following 3 control codes: [1F 1A], [1F 1C], and [1F F3]. What sprites appear can be manipulated by changing the sprite groups involved, which are listed in brackets. They seem to always use the sprite frame in the "up" position (top left corner of sprite group).

    01 = 3 White Surprise Lines (Above Head) [Sprite Group 218]
    02 = Walkie Talkie (From Captain Strong) [Sprite Group 352]
    03 = Exclamation Point Word Bubble [Sprite Group 232]
    04 = Light Bulb [Sprite Group 194]
    05 = Sky Walker Lightning Sparks [Sprite Group 421]
    06 = Happy People Flag (unused) [Sprite Group 366]
    07 = Nice's Head (Drummer from Runaway Brothers) This on is off to the left [Sprite Group 448]
    08 = Nice's Head (Drummer from Runaway Brothers) This one is off to the right [Sprite Group 448]
    09 = Heart (unused) [Sprite Group 351]
    0A = Bus Driver's Head (This on is off to the left) [Sprite Group 448]
    0B = Bus Driver's Head (This one is off to the right) [Sprite Group 448]
    0C and beyond = Glitches the game
