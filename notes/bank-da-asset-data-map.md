## Semantic Subcontracts

`notes/da-map-palette-subrecord-contracts.md` promotes the DA map palette
payload run and pointer tail into explicit row contracts:

- `DA:7CA7..DA:FAA6`: `DA_MAP_PALETTE_VARIANT_TABLE`, 168 physical rows of
  192 bytes. Each row is six 16-colour SNES BGR555 subpalettes for descriptor
  palettes `2..7`; the first words of subpalettes `0..3` are raw-ROM metadata
  slots named `event_flag`, `event_palette_selector_word`, `sprite_palette`,
  and `flash_effect`.
- `DA:FAA7..DA:FB06`: `MAP_PALETTE_POINTER_TABLE`, 32 three-byte long-pointer
  rows. Each pointer targets the corresponding `MAP_DATA_PALETTE_N` asset.

The subrecord contract is backed by `notes/map-fts-palette-variant-contract.md`,
`notes/map-palette-descriptor-context.md`, and
`notes/map-palette-command-usage-contract.md`: all 168 `.fts` row keys match the
DA palette settings, all raw-ROM versus visual-row differences are explained by
reserved metadata-word zeroing, descriptor palettes `2..7` fit the six DA
subpalettes with zero overflow cells, and every parsed `CHANGE_MAP_PALETTE`
command resolves to a DA palette id/variant pair.
